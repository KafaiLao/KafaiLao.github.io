About

I am a second-year master student in Electrical and Computer Engineering department at University of Macau, advised by Dr. Feng Wan. I am also teaching assistant of the course Digital Systems (Fall 2014 and 2015) and Power Electronics (Spring 2015).

I am working on the classification of Electroencephalography (EEG) signal ,especially for its application on the brain-computer interfaces (BCI). The research goal is to enhance the classification accuracy by employing multivariate statistical methods and machine learning techniques.
